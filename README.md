# Tower of Sin

**Developers:** Evelyn Johnson, Mario Tinoco, and Soham Hisabia.<br>
Created in Spring 2026 in CS 426 - Video Game Design and Development at the University of Illinois Chicago.

### Concept

**Idea:** This is a 3D action-roguelike dungeon crawler where players ascend the Tower of Sin, battling enemies and bosses (like Greed, Pride and Wrath), scaling as the player progresses, until they fail and must start from the beginning again. The further you progress in your run before you die, the more you gain for your next attempt.

**Lore:** You are a condemned soul. In order to prove you’ve been redeemed, you must traverse the Tower of Sin and defeat humanity’s fatal flaws: pride, greed, lust, envy, gluttony, wrath, and sloth. Only by climbing high enough to reach the end of the tower, can you truly escape.

Can **YOU** prove your redemption?

## Key Formal Elements

* **Players:** Single-player Vs. Game
* **Objectives:** Objective is mostly Capture (defeat enemies/bosses, clear the dungeon). There are some aspects of Exploration (unique runs, randomized mini boss types, basic enemy spawns, loot, exits, bosses), and Outwit (armor/weapons, learn attack patterns, dodge, use skills).
* **Audience:** Audience: Mid-core gamers who enjoy action-roguelikes, dungeon crawling combat, unique bosses/boss mechanics, and high-replayability loops (think: Hades 2, Minecraft Dungeons, Cult of the Lamb enjoyers).
* **Static Boundaries, Dynamic Rules:** Floors are structurally identical, but the gameplay stays fresh by randomizing the rules, procedures, debuffs, exits, enemy/loot spawns, and loot drops. 
* **Risk vs. Reward Encounters:** Mini-bosses do not block the exits. Players must choose whether to rush past the boss to the exit to escape quickly or risk their limited HP by fighting for the loot. 
* **Unique Bosses and Boss Mechanics:** Sin Bosses feature modifiers that can change the physical/visual properties of the room, the player, and other unique mechanics (e.g., spawn a copy of itself). For example, the "Sloth" boss increases the player's gravity and decreases their speed.
* **RNG Loot System:** Loot drops rely on RNG, where items have base stats modified by a rarity multiplier and an additional RNG modifier. This means that you will rarely get the same item, of the same rarity, with the same exact stats.
* **Meaningful Progression:** The game features permadeath where all run progress and loot are wiped if HP reaches zero. However, players return to the "Realm of Souls" (inspired by the Asphodel Meadows) to view cosmetic "Trophies" and equip permanent, stat-boosting "Titles".
